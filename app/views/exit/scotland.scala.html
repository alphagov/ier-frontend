Electoral registration form for Scotland

To update your details on the electoral register in Scotland you need to complete an electoral registration form.

To register to vote, or to update your details if you have moved or changed your name, download and print the registration form [PDF, 20KB].

You can also download and print the young voter registration form [PDF, 22KB] for the Scottish independence referendum.

You can find more information on the About My Vote website.
