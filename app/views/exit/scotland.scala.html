Voter registration forms for Scotland

You can't register online if you live in Scotland.

Instead, complete a paper form – then post it to your local Electoral Registration Office.

  • Voter registration form (Scotland)
  • Voter registration form (British citizens living abroad)
  • Voter registration form (armed forces)
  • Voter registration form (Crown servants and British Council employees)

Or complete the young voter registration form if you'll be 16 when the Scottish independence referendum takes place (18 September 2014).
