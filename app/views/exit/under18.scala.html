Sorry, you can’t register to vote without a date of birth

Try to find out your date of birth: you can only register to vote at 16 or 17 if you can provide an exact date of birth.

Or try to register again once you’re 18. You can register at 18 without an exact date of birth, as long as you can provide a genuine reason why you don’t know it.
