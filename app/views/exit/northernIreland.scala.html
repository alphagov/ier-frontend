Electoral registration form for Northern Ireland

To update your details on the electoral register in Northern Ireland you need to complete a registration canvass form.

To register to vote, or to update your details if you have moved or changed your name, download and print the registration canvass form [PDF, 12KB].

You can find more information on the Electoral Office for Northern Ireland website.
