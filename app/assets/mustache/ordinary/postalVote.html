Most people vote in person at a polling station. But if you prefer to vote by post, we can send you an application form.

How should we send you the form?

What is a postal vote?

With a postal vote, your ballot paper is sent to an address that you choose. You mark your vote and then return your ballot paper in the post.

Anyone can apply for a postal vote and you don't need to give a reason.

If you are not sure whether you want a postal vote, you can complete your registration now and then apply for a postal vote later.
